How roles work. Each role is just a checklist of permissions. To make a thin role like NE more capable, find it in the matrix and tick the permissions you want it to have. To restrict a role, untick. Changes apply immediately and are cached for 60s. The Super Admin column is locked at * (all permissions). Use + New Role to create a custom role, then assign permissions to it. Click Verify & Migrate only if the schema or default seed needs to be (re)created.
Tick / untick to grant / revoke. Roles can be delegated by Super Admin to anyone with the roles.assign permission.
