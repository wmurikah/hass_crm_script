Privacy Policy

Hass Petroleum Group Privacy Policy — Customer • Effective 2025 • Approved by the Chief Executive Officer

What We Collect

Hass Petroleum collects personal data including your name, email address, phone number, company details, and transaction records to operate the customer portal and provide fuel ordering and delivery services.

How We Use Your Data

  • To process orders, arrange deliveries, and send service updates
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the customer portal
  • To respond to support queries, complaints, and account requests
  • To authenticate users and maintain security of your account
  • To comply with applicable data protection laws in Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, South Sudan, and Zambia

Data Sharing

Your personal data will not be sold or disclosed to third parties except where required to deliver our services (such as logistics partners for deliveries), to comply with legal obligations, or with your prior consent.

Your Rights

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with Hass or the relevant data protection authority

Data Security

Hass Petroleum implements access controls and security measures to protect your personal information. Our customer service agents will never ask for your password.
